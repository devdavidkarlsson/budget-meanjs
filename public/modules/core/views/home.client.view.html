Accounts

Accounts Here you add your accounts, savings accounts, credit, debit and others. When you have added some incomes and expenses you can look here to make sure you are not running out of funds.

Categories

Categories Here you create categories to use in your budget. You can create any category you need. When you have added some expenses you can verify that you are not out of budget here

Incomes

Incomes Here you add your incomes and tie them to the corresponding accounts.

Expenses

Expenses Here you add your expenses, the expenses are tied both to accounts and categories.
